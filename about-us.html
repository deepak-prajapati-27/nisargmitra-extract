About Us

Established in 2016, BRM Chemicals is a proud sister concern of BRM Enterprises, which has been serving the industry since 1987. BRM Enterprises began its journey in Old Delhi, and over three decades, we realized that the demand for high-quality materials in smaller packs extended far beyond the city, reaching every corner of India. This insight led to the founding of BRM Chemicals in 2016.

In just six years, BRM Chemicals has grown significantly, successfully fulfilling over 10,000 orders and expanding our reach by exporting materials to seven countries.

Our Mission

We are committed to establishing BRM Chemicals as a renowned name in the raw material supply chain for the personal care and cosmetic industries. Our goal is to make affordable, high-quality raw materials accessible to everyone, and to inspire today’s generation, especially in India, to achieve great heights and leave their mark on the global business landscape.

Our Team

We are a dynamic team of 20 vibrant and energetic individuals, with more joining us every month as we continue to grow and expand our reach.

Word from our founder

Since our inception, we have grown from a small team with a big vision into a company that serves thousands worldwide. Our story is one of perseverance, innovation, and an unwavering commitment to excellence.
